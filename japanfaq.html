Visa Notes5

  • Visa Agency to Submit: Attic Tours (Davao Ecoland)
  1. Submit Visa Application at least 3 months before the Trip.
    1. In this case, February 3rd-4th week
  2. If possible, submit as one but no need all to be present. Just one person with letter of authority. (i.e This is to authorize XXXX to submit my visa requirements etc)
  3. Visa Type: Tourist.
  4. Can be Single Entry or Multiple Entry.
    1. Multiple Entry requirements are the same with Single Entry with an additional form to fill up and additional charge.
    2. Optional but better to apply Multiple Entry for future travels.
  5. Invitation Letter not needed. That is for Visa Type: Visiting Relatives. You are applying for Visa Type: Tourist.
  6. Certificate of Eligibility not needed. You are going as a tourist. Not as a worker.
  7. Itinerary for Visa Application:
  8. Visa Document Notes:
    1. NSO Documents (i.e. Birth, Cenomar, etc) should be retrieved within 1 year of VISA application. Not 2 years. Not 3 years. even if nothing has changed. You will be auto denied if you don't comply. There is a bar code in the document to determine the period of retrieval. Do not attempt send more than a year old NSO document.
    2. ADB 6 Months. No specific amount but 10k per day if possible for consertive estimate.
    3. Address the ADB to the Japan Embassy (PH Office)
      1. Embassy of Japan in the Philippines
      2. 2627, 1300 Roxas Blvd, Pasay, Metro Manila
      3. (02) 8551 5710
